GIANYROX

Pirate-tavern typography × Tron / Daft Punk execution. The strokes are blades.

The Mark

The Gianyrox G is built from machete-blade silhouettes — each stroke is a tapered blade form, sharp-pointed at the tip, wider at the base. Hairline mirror-polished edge highlights along every cutting edge. Neon orange on pitch black with a cyan glow.

Do. Use on solid black or very dark backgrounds. Orange is the primary; cyan glow is supporting. Hairline mirror highlight stays. Tilt is forbidden — the mark stands upright.
Don't. Re-color the orange. Add stroke. Rotate. Composite over busy photography. Modify the blade silhouettes. Add cursive script flourishes (the previous era's mistake).
