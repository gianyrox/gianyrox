Subject: PhD inquiry – generative models for protein ensemble prioritization

I was captivated by your BioEmu work demonstrating how generative deep learning can emulate protein equilibrium ensembles at remarkable speed—effectively launching an "emulation revolution" in structural biology beyond classical simulation.

Could transformer-based models trained on sequence-structure-function relationships guide prioritization of these ensemble outputs for experimental validation?
